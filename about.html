Who we are

REXORIA is a female fronted melodic metal band from the city Jönköping in southern of Sweden. The band was formed in the beginning of 2016 by Frida, Martin and Jonas. Soon they also found Cristofer who wanted to join their musical journey.

In april 2016 REXORIA released their first EP “Moments of Insanity” which contents four tracks. After a few weeks after the EP was released, they also released a music video with one of the songs. The response from the release got big from the start. After a few months the third track “Moonlight (Reach the Sky)” was shared by Spotify Sweden in one of their playlists. The biggest radio rock station in Sweden “Rockklassiker” played the song as well. Upon this, the vocalist Frida managed to be one of 15 people in Sweden to compete for the great price: “Rock Voice 2016”.

Right now the band are going to have gigs out there and promise all of you people and fans to give you a show you will never forget! So if you like powerful female vocals with melodic melodies, burning guitar riffs and blasting drums you really should check out or contact REXORIA.
